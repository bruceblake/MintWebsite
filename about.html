About Mint

Meet Sebas: The Passion Behind Mint Detailing

Welcome to Mint Detailing, where passion meets precision. I'm Sebas, the founder and lead detailer behind Mint, and I've built this business on a foundation of genuine enthusiasm for automotive care and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

My Journey

My fascination with cars began at an early age. What started as helping my father maintain our family vehicles quickly evolved into a passion for bringing out the best in every automobile I encountered. After years of learning, practicing, and refining my techniques, I decided to transform this passion into a profession that would allow me to share my expertise with fellow car enthusiasts and owners.

The Mint Philosophy

At Mint, we believe that every vehicle deserves exceptional care. Whether it's a daily driver or a weekend showpiece, we approach each project with the same level of dedication and attention to detail. Our name "Mint" represents our commitment to returning your vehicle to pristine, mint condition through meticulous detailing and care.

Our Approach

What sets Mint apart is our personalized approach to detailing. We take the time to understand your specific needs and concerns, developing a tailored plan that addresses your vehicle's unique requirements. We don't just clean cars; we rejuvenate them, using premium products and cutting-edge techniques to achieve results that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional detailing services that not only enhance the appearance of our clients' vehicles but also protect their investment and provide a sense of pride in ownership.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in vehicle detailing, known for uncompromising quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Certifications

We're committed to maintaining the highest standards in the detailing industry. Our team continuously pursues education and certification to ensure we're offering the best techniques and products available.

Ceramic Pro Certified Installer

Paint Correction Specialist
